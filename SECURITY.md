# Security policy

## Reporting

Do not open a public issue containing credentials, private data, or an exploitable vulnerability. Contact the repository maintainer privately through the competition team profile.

## Demonstration boundaries

- Synthetic data only.
- Read-only MCP policy tools.
- No biometric, passport-number, national-ID, sanctions, or live screening integrations.
- No autonomous external actions.
- No secrets committed to source control.
- Elevated and uncertain cases require a human decision.

See `docs/security.md` for the threat model and implemented controls.
