# Product specification

## Problem

Small organisations often handle visitor requests through fragmented paper, spreadsheet, chat, and verbal processes. This makes routing inconsistent and leaves weak evidence for later review.

## User

A front-desk officer or authorised supervisor reviewing synthetic visitor requests.

## Product promise

GateTrack Sentinel validates requests, blocks unsafe inputs, transparently routes risk, retrieves relevant policy, prepares a bounded recommendation, pauses elevated cases for a human, and records the full trace.

## Non-goals

The capstone is not a production access-control, legal, sanctions, identity-verification, or regulatory decision system.
